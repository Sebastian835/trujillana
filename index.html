Our Company

Who We Are

Trujillana Multi Service Solutions is a Maryland-based property service company led by Florinda Rojas. Our company was built on responsibility, hard work, and a strong commitment to delivering dependable results.

We understand that maintaining and improving a property requires versatility and attention to detail. That is why we offer complete multi-service solutions tailored to homeowners, property managers, and businesses throughout Silver Spring and surrounding areas.

Our goal is to provide efficient, organized, and high-quality services that increase property value, improve appearance, and ensure long-term functionality.

Our Mission

Provide reliable and professional property services with integrity, efficiency, and attention to detail — ensuring customer satisfaction in every project.

Our Vision

Become a trusted multi-service company in Maryland, recognized for versatility, quality workmanship, and consistent results.
